About Maku

Maku brings you the classic taste of pizzas and burgers in a retro setting. Our recipes are inspired by the golden era of diners, with a modern twist!

Step into Maku and you'll be transported to a vibrant 90s burger joint founded in 1995, complete with neon lights, checkered floors, and the irresistible aroma of fresh-baked pizza and sizzling burgers. We pride ourselves on using only the freshest ingredients, hand-tossed dough, and juicy, flame-grilled patties. Whether you're here for a family dinner, a quick lunch, or a late-night snack, our friendly staff and cozy booths make every visit memorable.

At Maku, we believe great food brings people together. Enjoy our signature pizzas, towering burgers, and classic sides in a fun, nostalgic atmosphere. Welcome to your new favorite spot in town!
